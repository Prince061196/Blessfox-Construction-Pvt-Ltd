Our Milestones

Since our founding in 2024, we have steadily grown from servicing individual clients to working with large corporates. We started with small projects and, through the quality of our services, gained trust from big clients and became involved in major projects.


2024

We started off by offering our services to individual clients. We were involved in small to medium projects. During this phase we gained vast experience in the construction industry.
